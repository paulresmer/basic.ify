about

underground.ify is a tool that allows users to examine the uniqueness of their music taste; i.e., how "underground" they are.

faq

how is my grade calculated?

your grade is calculated by averaging the popularity indices of your 10 most listened-to artists over the last six months. each artist's popularity index is based, for the most part, on the total number of plays their tracks have had and how recent those plays are.

my top artists don't look right! what happened?

undergroundify accesses data collected by Spotify through calls to the Spotify API. since these data collection methods are not available to the public, it is impossible to say why certain users may experience discrepancies between their undergroundify results and their actual listening history. it is worth noting that since a lot of data collected by Spotify is not updated to the API in real time, a few days of lag is common before your undergroundify data is updated.
